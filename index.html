Six QR codes placed throughout The Kitchen link to the 360-degree videos in the Invisible Art Handler series (Monitor, Mop, Ladder, Light, Cables, and Chop Saw) portraying standard maintenance tasks with the person enacting them missing from the frame.

The Invisible Art Handler Website adds a new layer to this project by making a series of art handling tools accessible in AR. Allowing viewers to handle these objects on their own terms, this site invites viewers to experiment with the creative potential of looking and touching, both within and outside of The Kitchen's building.
